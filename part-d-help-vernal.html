Part D Prescription Drug Plan Help in Vernal, Utah

Free Part D Plan Comparison for Vernal Residents

We help you find the Part D prescription drug plan with the lowest cost for your specific medications.

Part D plans vary widely in cost depending on which prescriptions you take and which pharmacy you use. We run your medication list through all available plans to find your best option in the Uintah Basin.

What We Check

  • Your Medications: We verify all your prescriptions are covered
  • Formulary Tiers: Check which tier your drugs are on (affects copays)
  • Pharmacy Network: Confirm your preferred Vernal pharmacy participates
  • Total Annual Cost: Calculate premium + deductible + copays
  • Coverage Gap: Explain how the "donut hole" affects you

When to Enroll in Part D

Initial Enrollment: 3 months before to 3 months after turning 65

Annual Enrollment Period: October 15 – December 7 each year

Special Enrollment: Certain life events may qualify
